Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Our products are designed with a "privacy-first" approach, ensuring your data remains yours.

Last Updated: May 2026

Service Overview

This privacy policy applies to our news-related ecosystem software, including our Browser Extensions and Mobile Applications. We provide real-time trending news and hot topic information efficiently while maintaining the highest standards of user privacy.

Data Collection

Our services do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information (PII). Your personal browsing or reading activity remains completely private.

User Data & Accounts

No user account, password, precise locations, or device tracking information is gathered. We do not analyze who you are or track what you browse outside of the application's core functions.

Capabilities & Permissions Usage

We only request specific permissions strictly necessary for rendering cross-platform content.

INTERNET (Android App)

Required for the mobile app to communicate securely with remote Cloudflare API endpoints and fetch the latest multi-language public news stream.

storage / Cache Management

Used to store local layout configurations and user UI language preferences. This data stays entirely on your local device.

alarms / Background Sync

Used to periodically fetch data updates to guarantee freshly populated feeds upon immediate client startup.

tabs / Custom CustomTabs

Invoked whenever you tap a feed item, triggering a native view container or browser tab to securely navigate to the original publisher's article source.

Secure Remote Infrastructure

Our ecosystem aggregates open-source content via programmatic API workers (e.g., Cloudflare serverless edge). The pipeline strictly fetches public payloads and completely avoids telemetry collection.

Third-Party Feeds

We fetch open RSS elements to compile a structured feed matrix. Clicking on individual feed entries redirects users to external publishers. We exert no tracking over those individual destinations.
