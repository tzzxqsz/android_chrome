隐私政策 (Privacy Policy)

本隐私政策适用于您使用由个人开发者（以下简称“我们”）开发的原生 Java 游戏《2048 经典消除》（以下简称“本应用”）。我们非常重视您的隐私，本隐私政策旨在向您说明本应用在保护您隐私方面的承诺。

1. 数据收集与使用

本应用是一款纯粹的单机游戏。我们不会收集您的任何个人信息。

  • 个人身份信息：我们不收集您的姓名、电话号码、电子邮件地址、设备标识符（如 IMEI、Android ID）或任何其他可用于识别您身份的信息。
  • 游戏数据存储：您的游戏得分、历史最高分（Best Score）等游戏进度数据仅存储在您设备的本地存储中（使用 SharedPreferences 技术）。这些数据完全由您个人控制，我们没有服务器，也无法访问、修改或上传这些数据。
  • 网络权限：本应用不需要也不使用互联网权限。它完全在离线状态下运行，确保没有任何数据离开您的设备。

2. 权限说明

本应用在运行过程中可能仅需要极少数保障基本游戏体验的系统权限，例如：

  • 震动权限 (VIBRATE)：仅用于在游戏合并或达成高分时为您提供触觉反馈。您可以随时在手机系统设置中关闭此权限。

3. 第三方服务与 SDK

本应用纯净无广告，没有集成任何第三方分析、广告或追踪 SDK（如 Google AdMob、Firebase 等）。因此，没有任何第三方会通过本应用收集您的任何行为或设备数据。

4. 儿童隐私保护

由于本应用不收集任何个人数据，它对所有年龄段（包括儿童）都是绝对安全的。我们鼓励父母或监护人监督儿童的移动应用使用习惯。

5. 隐私政策的更新

如果我们未来对游戏功能进行升级（例如加入网络排行榜或云存档），可能会适时更新本隐私政策。任何更新都将在此页面公布。

6. 联系我们

如果您对本隐私政策或应用有任何疑问，请通过您的应用商店开发者联系方式与我们取得联系。

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to your use of the native Java puzzle game "2048 Classic" (hereinafter referred to as "the Application"), developed by an individual developer (hereinafter referred to as "we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy, and this policy explains our practices.

1. Data Collection and Use

This Application is a pure offline single-player game. We do not collect any personal information.

  • Personal Data: We do not collect your name, phone number, email address, device identifiers (such as IMEI, Android ID), or any other information that can be used to identify you.
  • Game Data Storage: Your game scores and best records are stored exclusively on your local device (using Android SharedPreferences). This data is entirely under your control. We do not own any servers, and we have no ability to access, modify, or upload your data.
  • Network Permissions: This Application does not require or use internet/network permissions. It runs fully offline, ensuring no data ever leaves your device.

2. Permissions Explained

The Application may request minimal system permissions only to provide essential gameplay experiences:

  • Vibration (VIBRATE): Used solely to provide haptic feedback when tiles are merged or high scores are achieved. You can disable this in your system settings at any time.

3. Third-Party Services and SDKs

This Application is 100% ad-free and does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs (such as Google AdMob, Firebase, etc.). No third party will collect your behavior or device data through this app.

4. Children's Privacy

Since the Application does not collect any personal data, it is completely safe for users of all ages, including children. We encourage parents and guardians to supervise their children's mobile activities.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If we update the gameplay features in the future (e.g., adding online leaderboards), we may revise this Privacy Policy accordingly. Any changes will be posted on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the developer contact information provided in the app store.
